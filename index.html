Interesting Facts About Chameleons

  • Chameleons are reptiles that are part of the suborder
  • Changing skin color is an important part of communication among chameleons
  • Most Chameleons have a prehensile tail that they use to wrap around tree branches

Interesting facts about pigeons

Interesting facts about camels

  • Their humps let them store up to 80 pounds of fat which they can live off for weeks and even months!
  • Camels are very strong and can carry up to 900 pounds for 25 miles a day.
  • When a camel finally does find water, he can drink up to 40 gallons in one go.

Interesting facts about bears

  1. Bears eat mostly meat and fish, but some bears also eat plants and insects.
  2. Black bears can run at the speed up to 35mph.
  3. Canada is home to nearly 60 % of the world's polar bears.

Interesting facts about pandas

  • Giant pandas are good at climbing trees and can also swim.
  • Pandas have so many fans because they look cute.
  • You can see panda babies in August.
